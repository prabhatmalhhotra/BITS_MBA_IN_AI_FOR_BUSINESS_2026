**Course** **Description:** Fundamental concepts and principles of
management as applied to a variety of organizations; elementary study of
managerial roles, styles, activities and decision making; relationship
with organizational effectiveness; basic concepts relating to planning
activities, manpower development; basic concepts relating to
organizational behaviour

**Course Objectives**

|  |  |
|:---|:---|
| **No** | **Course Objective** |
| **CO1** | Understand the key principles of managing individuals and groups in organizations |
| **CO2** | Analyze the impact of leadership styles and organizational culture on performance |
| **CO3** | Apply motivational theories to enhance employee engagement |
| **CO4** | Develop strategies for effective communication and conflict resolution |
| **CO5** | Explore the influence of global and ethical considerations in managing people |

**<span class="mark">Experiential Learning Components:</span>**

**<u>In-class Experiential Learning</u>**

Wherever applicable, the course will be run using examples & case
studies related to OB & HR

**<u>Experiential Learning Assignment</u>**

This is an individual assignment.

Identify a **HR and/or OB** related problem faced by your organization
(no fictitious company is allowed), identify crucial theoretical
concepts **(related to the course)** that explain the cause(s) of the
problem and highlight the actions (initiatives, policies, programs)
implemented by your organization. For the chosen company, you are
required to carry out a comprehensive study using publicly available
resources as per the following format.

1\. Background

2\. Problem Statement

3\. Theoretical Causes of Problem

4\. Initiatives of company (To solve problem)

5\. Conclusion

6\. References (Please remember that search engine as Google is not a
reference, here you may provide URLs of those news/magazine articles or
company website from where you have collected the information)
