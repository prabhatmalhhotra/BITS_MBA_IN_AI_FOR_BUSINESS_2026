# Managerial Accounting — Syllabus

> Likely BITS course code: **MBA ZC415 / MBA ZG515** (varies). Verify on Taxila handout.

## Course objective

Equip future managers to read, analyse and use accounting information for decision-making — both **financial accounting** (external reporting) and **management accounting** (internal control, planning, decision support).

## Modules / units

### Module 1 — Introduction to Accounting
- Meaning, branches (Financial, Cost, Management)
- Users of accounting information; financial vs managerial accounting
- Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP); concepts and conventions
- Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS) overview

### Module 2 — Accounting Cycle
- Double-entry book-keeping; debit and credit rules
- Journal, ledger, posting
- Trial balance; rectification of errors
- Subsidiary books, cash book

### Module 3 — Financial Statements
- Trading account, profit & loss account (P&L)
- Balance sheet
- Schedule III format under Companies Act
- Adjustments: closing stock, depreciation, prepaid/outstanding, accruals
- Statement of cash flows: operating, investing, financing activities (direct & indirect method)

### Module 4 — Depreciation, Provisions & Reserves
- Depreciation methods: straight-line, written-down value, units-of-production
- AS-10 / Ind AS 16 essentials
- Provisions vs reserves; types of reserves

### Module 5 — Inventory Valuation
- Methods: FIFO, LIFO, weighted average
- AS-2 / Ind AS 2 — lower of cost or NRV

### Module 6 — Financial Statement Analysis
- Horizontal, vertical (common-size) and trend analysis
- Ratio analysis:
  - Liquidity: current, quick, cash
  - Solvency: debt-equity, interest-coverage
  - Profitability: GP, NP, ROCE, ROE
  - Efficiency: inventory turnover, debtors turnover, asset turnover
- Du Pont decomposition of ROE

### Module 7 — Cost Accounting Concepts
- Classification of costs: direct/indirect, fixed/variable/semi-variable, product/period
- Cost-Volume-Profit (CVP) analysis: contribution, P/V ratio, break-even point, margin of safety
- Operating leverage

### Module 8 — Marginal vs Absorption Costing
- Definitions; differences in profit reporting
- Reconciliation of profit
- Decision-making applications: make-or-buy, sell-or-process-further, accept-or-reject special order, drop-or-continue product line, key-factor analysis

### Module 9 — Budgeting and Budgetary Control
- Types of budgets: master, functional, fixed, flexible
- Cash budget; sales/production budget
- Zero-based budgeting; rolling budgets

### Module 10 — Standard Costing & Variance Analysis
- Material variances: price, usage, mix, yield
- Labour variances: rate, efficiency, idle-time
- Overhead variances: spending, efficiency, volume
- Sales variances (overview)

### Module 11 — Responsibility Accounting & Performance Measurement
- Cost centres, profit centres, investment centres
- Transfer pricing (overview)
- Balanced Scorecard (overview)

### Module 12 — Decision-making with Accounting Information
- Relevant cost analysis
- Capital budgeting basics: Payback, ARR, NPV, IRR (deeper coverage in Sem 2 Corporate Finance)
