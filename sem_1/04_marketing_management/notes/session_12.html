IMC, Advertising & Sales Promotion

Integrated Marketing Communications ensures every touchpoint tells the same story. Plus deep dives into advertising decisions and sales promotion tools.

T1 Chapters 12 & 13Case: Oreo India (R1 Case 17)Comprehensive Exam
Real-World Problem

How did Oreo become India's #1 cream biscuit in just 2 years?

When Mondelez launched Oreo in India in 2011, Britannia Treat and ITC Sunfeast already dominated cream biscuits. Oreo's weapon? Integrated Marketing Communications (IMC). TV ads taught the "Twist, Lick, Dunk" ritual. Digital campaigns (#OreoDunkSweepstakes) went viral. In-store sampling let people taste. Cricket tie-ups (IPL) built mass awareness. Every touchpoint reinforced ONE message: Oreo is fun. The Indian twist: the father-child bond angle (not mother-child, as in the West). Result: 30% of cream biscuit market within 2 years.

IMC is not about using many channels — it is about coordinated, consistent messaging across ALL channels. Oreo's success was in the integration, not just the spend.

2 Sales Promotion Intermediate

TypeToolsExample
ConsumerSamples, coupons, cash refunds, contests, free trialsFlipkart: "Buy 2 Get 1 Free", Swiggy: "Flat 50% off first order"
TradeDiscounts, allowances, free goodsFMCG companies giving retailers display allowances
Business/Sales ForceContests, trade shows, specialty advertisingAnnual dealer conferences, incentive trips

Pros of Sales Promotion

  • Short-term sales spike
  • Encourages product trial
  • Defends market share

Cons of Sales Promotion

  • Erodes brand image if overused
  • Attracts deal-prone, non-loyal customers
  • Can start price wars

Trending Example (2024-25)

Influencer marketing explosion: Mamaearth spends 35%+ of revenue on influencer/digital marketing. Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) deliver higher engagement than celebrities. Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts are the primary formats. D2C brands like Plum, mCaffeine, and Mokobara built entirely through digital-first IMC.

Exam Tip: The Oreo India case (R1 Case 17) is the mandatory case discussion. Be ready to analyse: (1) How did Oreo use IMC across TV, digital, in-store, and events? (2) What was the localisation strategy (father-child bond)? (3) Why was integration the key, not just heavy spending? Also watch HST8 on Sustainable Advertising.

Session 12 — Key Takeaways Checklist

  • IMC = coordinated, consistent messaging across ALL channels and touchpoints
  • 5M of Advertising: Mission, Money, Message, Media, Measurement
  • GRP = Reach × Frequency
  • Sales promotion: Consumer, Trade, Business — effective short-term but erodes brand if overused
  • POEM model: Paid + Owned + Earned media
  • Oreo India case: IMC masterclass — Twist Lick Dunk + father-child bond + digital + sampling
  • ASCI Code regulates advertising in India — surrogate ads for alcohol/tobacco restricted
