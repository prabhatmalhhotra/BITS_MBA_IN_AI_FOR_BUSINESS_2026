# Marketing Management — One-Page Revision Sheet

## Core
Need → Want → Demand. Value = Benefits ÷ Costs. Marketing ≠ Selling.

## Holistic marketing dimensions
Relationship · Integrated · Internal · Performance.

## Environment
**PESTEL**: Political · Economic · Social · Technological · Environmental · Legal.
**Porter 5F**: New entrants · Suppliers · Buyers · Substitutes · Rivalry.

## Research
Primary vs Secondary; Qualitative vs Quantitative; Probability vs Non-probability sampling.

## Consumer behaviour
5 stages: Need → Search → Evaluation → Purchase → Post-purchase.
Influences: Cultural · Social · Personal · Psychological.
Theory of Planned Behaviour: Intention = Attitude + Norms + Perceived Control.

## B2B
Buying centre: Initiator · User · Influencer · Decider · Buyer · Gatekeeper.
Situations: New task · Modified rebuy · Straight rebuy.

## STP
**Segmentation bases**: Demographic · Geographic · Psychographic · Behavioural · Technographic.
**MASDA**: Measurable · Accessible · Substantial · Differentiable · Actionable.
**Targeting**: Undifferentiated · Differentiated · Concentrated · Micro.
**Positioning**: POPs (must-haves) · PODs (distinctive value) · Statement template.

## Product
**5 levels**: Core · Basic · Expected · Augmented · Potential.
**Mix**: Width · Length · Depth · Consistency.
**PLC**: Introduction → Growth → Maturity → Decline.
**NPD (8 stages)**: Idea → Screen → Concept → Strategy → Business analysis → Develop → Test → Commercialise.

## Brand equity
**Aaker**: Awareness · Quality · Associations · Loyalty · Other.
**Keller CBBE**: Salience → Performance + Imagery → Judgements + Feelings → Resonance.

## Pricing
**Methods**: Cost-plus · Target return · Value-based · Competition · Sealed-bid · Dynamic · Auction.
**Strategies**: Skim · Penetrate · Premium · Economy · Psychological · Bundle · Captive · Two-part · Promotional.
PED = %ΔQ / %ΔP.

## Place
**Channel levels**: 0 (D2C) · 1 · 2 · 3.
**Intensity**: Intensive · Selective · Exclusive.
**VMS**: Corporate · Contractual · Administered.

## Promotion / IMC
**Models**: AIDA · Hierarchy of Effects · Funnel.
**Tools**: Advertising · Sales promotion · PR · Personal selling · Direct & digital · Events · Social/Influencer.
**Metrics**: Reach, Frequency, GRP, CPM, CPC, CTR, CAC, LTV, ROAS.

**Digital channels**: SEO · SEM · Social · Content · Influencer · Email · Affiliate · Display.

## Services (IIVP)
Intangibility · Inseparability · Variability · Perishability.
**7Ps** = 4Ps + People · Process · Physical evidence.
**SERVQUAL (RATER)**: Reliability · Assurance · Tangibles · Empathy · Responsiveness.
**5 gaps** between expectations and perceptions.

## Strategy
**CLV** = Σ (Margin × Retention^t)/(1+r)^t − CAC.
**Ansoff**: Penetration · Market development · Product development · Diversification.
**BCG**: Stars · Cash cows · Question marks · Dogs.

## AI in marketing
- Customer 360 + propensity models
- Recommendation engines (collab/content/hybrid)
- GenAI for content, copy, images, video
- Conversational AI for support + lead qualification
- Marketing Mix Modelling (MMM) + Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA)
- Privacy: DPDP Act 2023; bias and fairness audits

## Indian challenger brands to cite
Cred · Nykaa · boAt · Mamaearth · Sugar · Zomato · Swiggy · OnePlus · Royal Enfield.

## Last-minute case answer template

1. Define problem in one line.
2. Apply 1–2 frameworks explicitly.
3. STP recommendation.
4. 4P or IMC plan with specifics + ₹ figures.
5. KPIs and measurement plan.
6. Risks and mitigation.
