## Marketing fundamentals

- **Definition (Kotler)**: Activity, set of institutions and processes for creating, communicating, delivering and exchanging offerings of value.
- **Marketing concept** (outside-in) ≠ Selling concept (inside-out).
- **4 Ps (McCarthy)**: Product, Price, Place, Promotion. **7 Ps for services**: + People, Process, Physical evidence.
- **Holistic marketing**: Internal + Integrated + Performance + Relationship.
- **New realities (Kotler)**: Technology, Globalisation, Social responsibility.

## Need-Want-Demand

| Concept | Definition |
|---------|-----------|
| Need | Basic requirement (food, safety) |
| Want | Need shaped by culture/personality |
| Demand | Want backed by ability to pay |

**Maslow** — Physiological → Safety → Social → Esteem → Self-actualisation.
**Demand states**: Negative, No demand, Latent, Declining, Irregular, Full, Overfull, Unwholesome.

## Consumer behaviour

- **Buying decision (5-stage)**: Need recognition → Information search → Evaluation → Purchase → Post-purchase.
- **Influencing factors**: Cultural, Social, Personal, Psychological.
- **Cognitive dissonance**: Post-purchase doubt; manage with reassurance and easy returns.
- **Black-box / EKB model**: stimuli → buyer's box → response.

## Competitive analysis

- **Porter 5 forces**: Rivalry, New entrants, Substitutes, Supplier power, Buyer power.
- **Strategic group**: Firms with similar strategy on key dimensions.
- **SWOT, PESTLE** for environment scan.

## STP

- **Segmentation bases (B2C)**: Geographic, Demographic, Psychographic, Behavioural.
- **Segmentation bases (B2B)**: Demographic (industry, size), Operating, Purchasing approach, Situational, Personal.
- **Effective segmentation**: Measurable, Substantial, Accessible, Differentiable, Actionable.
- **Targeting**: Undifferentiated, Differentiated, Concentrated (niche), Micromarketing.
- **Personas**: Semi-fictional ideal customer profile per segment (HSTalk: Annmarie Hanlon).

## Customer Value Proposition (CVP)

- **CVP statement**: For (target), who (need), our (offering) is a (category) that (benefit), unlike (competitor), our (differentiator).
- **VP Canvas** = Customer Profile (jobs/pains/gains) ↔ Value Map (products, pain relievers, gain creators).
- **Customer-perceived value** = Total benefits − Total costs.

## Positioning

- **Components**: Target segment + Frame of reference + POD + RTB.
- **POP** (must-have) vs **POD** (unique advantage).
- **Errors**: Underpositioning, Overpositioning, Confused, Doubtful.
- **Tool**: Perceptual map.

## Product

- **5 levels (Kotler)**: Core benefit → Basic → Expected → Augmented → Potential.
- **PLC**: Introduction → Growth → Maturity → Decline (each with distinct strategy).
- **NPD**: Idea generation → Screening → Concept dev/test → Marketing strategy → Business analysis → Product dev → Test market → Commercialisation.
- **Differentiation**: Product, Service, Personnel, Channel, Image.

## Services marketing

- **IHIP characteristics**: Intangibility, Heterogeneity (Variability), Inseparability, Perishability.
- **SERVQUAL (RATER)**: Reliability, Assurance, Tangibles, Empathy, Responsiveness.
- **Service blueprint**: Map customer journey + front-stage/back-stage processes.
- **Service-Profit Chain**: Internal SQ → Employee sat → Productivity → Customer value → Customer sat & loyalty → Revenue.

## Pricing

- **6-step process**: Objective → Demand → Cost → Competitor → Method → Final price.
- **Methods**: Markup, Target-return, Perceived-value, Value (EDLP), Going-rate, Auction.
- **Penetration** (low-price, share-grab) vs **Skimming** (high-price, premium segment).
- **Elasticity** = % Δ Q / % Δ P; |E| > 1 elastic.
- **EDLP** vs **High-Low**.
- **Geographic pricing**: FOB origin, Uniform delivered, Zone, Freight-absorption.
- **Discounts**: Cash, Quantity, Functional, Seasonal; Allowances (trade-in, promotional).

## Marketing Communications (IMC)

- **8 modes (Kotler)**: Advertising, Sales Promotion, Events & Experiences, PR & Publicity, Direct & Database, Online & Social, Mobile, Personal Selling.
- **Macro-model**: Sender → Encoding → Message/Media → Decoding → Receiver → Response → Feedback (Noise everywhere).
- **AIDA**: Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action.
- **Hierarchy of effects**: Awareness → Knowledge → Liking → Preference → Conviction → Purchase.
- **Steps**: Identify audience → Set objectives → Design message → Choose channels → Set budget → Decide mix → Measure → Manage IMC.
- **Budgeting**: Affordable, % of sales, Competitive parity, **Objective-and-task** (preferred).

## Advertising & Sales Promotion

- **5 Ms**: Mission, Money, Message, Media, Measurement.
- **Objectives**: Inform, Persuade, Remind, Reinforce.
- **Reach × Frequency × Impact** = GRPs (Gross Rating Points).
- **Consumer SP**: Coupons, Samples, Cash refunds, Premiums, Loyalty, Contests, POP.
- **Trade SP**: Allowances, Free goods, Co-op ads, Sales contests.

## Channels of Distribution

- **Functions**: Information, Promotion, Negotiation, Ordering, Financing, Risk-taking, Physical possession, Payment, Title transfer.
- **Levels**: 0 (direct) / 1 / 2 / 3.
- **Design steps**: Customer needs → Objectives → Alternatives → Evaluate (3 criteria: economic, control, adaptive).
- **VMS**: Corporate, Administered, Contractual (incl. Franchise).
- **Conflict**: Vertical (between levels), Horizontal (same level), Multichannel.

## E/M/Quick-Commerce

- **E-commerce**: Internet-based txns on any device.
- **M-commerce**: Subset, mobile devices/apps.
- **Q-commerce**: 10-min delivery (Blinkit, Zepto, Instamart) — dark stores, hub-and-spoke.
- **Disintermediation**: Bypass intermediaries (D2C).
- **Reintermediation**: New intermediaries replace old.
- **Omnichannel**: Seamless integrated experience (HSTalk: Atorough).

## Marketing Regulation (India)

| Regulator | Scope |
|-----------|-------|
| ASCI | Advertising standards (self-regulatory) |
| CCPA | Consumer Protection Act 2019, e-commerce rules |
| TRAI | Telecom |
| FSSAI | Food safety |
| CCI | Competition |
| IRDAI | Insurance |
| SEBI | Securities marketing |

- **Recent areas**: Greenwashing (ASCI 2024), Influencer disclosure (#ad), AI-generated content / deepfakes.

## Market Research & B2B

- **MR steps**: Define problem → Plan → Collect → Analyse → Present → Decide.
- **Primary** (survey, observation, experiment) vs **Secondary** (govt, internal, syndicated).
- **B2B characteristics**: Derived demand, fewer larger buyers, multi-stakeholder, professional buyers, longer cycle, geographic concentration.
- **Buying centre roles (Webster & Wind)**: Initiator, User, Influencer, Decider, Approver, Buyer, Gatekeeper.
- **B2B strategy**: Account-based marketing (ABM), customised content per role.

## Marketing Strategy & Plan

- **Marketing plan**: Exec summary → Situation analysis (SWOT/PESTLE) → Marketing strategy (STP, 4Ps) → Action programs → Budgets → Controls.
- **BCG matrix**: Market Growth × Relative Market Share → Star, Cash Cow, Question Mark, Dog.
- **GE / McKinsey**: Industry attractiveness × Business strength (3×3).
- **Ansoff Growth**: Market Penetration / Market Development / Product Development / Diversification.
- **CLV** = NPV of profit stream over customer lifetime.
