# Corporate Finance — PYQ Bank

## Mid-Sem Paper A — 60 marks
1. (5 m) State three differences between profit and wealth maximisation.
2. (5 m) Compute FV of ₹2,00,000 at 9% for 8 years compounded quarterly.
3. (8 m) Beta of stock = 1.5; Rf = 7%; Rm = 13%. Cost of equity? If beta rises to 1.8, what is the new cost?
4. (8 m) Bond face value ₹1,000, coupon 8%, maturity 5 years, YTM 10%. Compute price.
5. (10 m) NPV / IRR / PI for a 4-year project. (Numerical given.)
6. (10 m) Discuss capital budgeting. Why is NPV preferred over IRR for mutually exclusive projects?
7. (14 m) WACC + capital-structure decision case.

## Mid-Sem Paper B — 50 marks
1. (5 m) Define agency problem and three mitigation mechanisms.
2. (5 m) Compute PV of perpetuity of ₹50,000 at 10%.
3. (10 m) DCF valuation of a stable-growth firm using DDM.
4. (10 m) Capital budgeting: 3 mutually exclusive projects; rank by NPV and IRR.
5. (10 m) Compute WACC: given E, D, P, costs and tax.
6. (10 m) Discuss CAPM. Limitations of CAPM in emerging markets like India.

## Comprehensive Paper A — 100 marks
1. (10 m) Discuss capital structure theories with diagrams.
2. (10 m) DOL/DFL/DCL computation + interpretation.
3. (10 m) Working capital + CCC + EOQ numerical.
4. (10 m) Dividend policy: Walter, Gordon, MM compared.
5. (15 m) DCF (FCFF) valuation case — multi-year forecast + terminal value.
6. (15 m) M&A: synergy + premium + accretion-dilution analysis.
7. (15 m) Theory: Behavioural finance + debiasing.
8. (15 m) Mini-case: capital-budgeting decision under uncertainty (sensitivity + scenario).

## Comprehensive Paper B — 80 marks
1. (10 m) International finance: forex risk + hedging mechanisms.
2. (10 m) Project NPV with inflation + risk-adjusted discount rate.
3. (10 m) Cost of capital for a project of different risk than firm — adjusted WACC.
4. (10 m) Working capital case: comparing aggressive vs conservative strategy financially.
5. (15 m) M&A case with detailed valuation.
6. (10 m) ESG and sustainable finance — green bonds + climate risk.
7. (15 m) Capital structure decision: optimal D/E mix given EBIT-EPS analysis.

## Tips
- Carry your own formula sheet (TVM, CAPM, WACC, capital budgeting, leverage).
- Show every formula and substitution step — partial credit on logic.
- Always recommend with a sentence of business reasoning at the end.
